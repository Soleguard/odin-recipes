Ground Turkey

Description

Ground turkey is beneficial for one's daily protein, containing plain grounded meat.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Turkey

Steps

  1. Pour oil onto pan
  2. Set stove to 2
  3. Carefully place ground turkey onto pan
  4. Add salt and black pepper
  5. Intermittently mash ground turkey for 15-20 minutes
