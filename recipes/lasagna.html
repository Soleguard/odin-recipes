Lasagna

Description

A slice of lasagna boasts of several ingredients. While some may see the dish as simple cheese, tomato sauce, and pasta, it is more than these.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzerella
  • Meatballs
  • Roman Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cook meat sauce
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Prepare cheese
  5. Pour ingredients into pan
  6. Set pan into oven for heating
